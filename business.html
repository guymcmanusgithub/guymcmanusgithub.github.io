Strategy

Business strategy is the process of researching the three levels of business (Environment, Market and Organisation) and then designing tactics to provide your organisation with choice.

Operations

Operations are the processes that your run your organisations. They focus on three main areas: 1) Your People; 2) Processes; 3) Products

Marketing

Marketing can be broken into four areas:
1. Researching your market.
2. Designing strategies.
3. The Marketing Mix.
4. Implementing, Managing and Measuring
